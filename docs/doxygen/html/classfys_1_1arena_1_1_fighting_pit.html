Detailed Description

A fighting pit represent a specific instance of a battle.

A fightingpit is composed of:

  • a layout (mapping of how the monsters/players are spread on the board)
  • a PitContenders object managing all the monsters and their behaviours (scripting)
  • a AllyPartyTeams object managing the players, each client has a PartyTeam (multiple party teams possible for one AllyPartyTeams) and the PartyTeam is composed of TeamMember that are the actual characters spread on the gaming board.
  • The 18 instances of SideBattle
