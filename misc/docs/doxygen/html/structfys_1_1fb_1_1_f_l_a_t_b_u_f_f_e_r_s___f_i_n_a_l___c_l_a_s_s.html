Detailed Description

Used by the client to do an action.

Reply frame sent to the player.

Used by the WorldServer to trigger new encounter/join to an ArenaServer.

Used as reply from the ArenaServer to the client connection_str is used by the client as authentication on the ArenaServer

Used by the client to join an existing FightingPit (id_fightingpit set) ALSO Used by the client as an authentication frame for messages
